About the Game

SAGA OF KALKI 2898 AD is an action-packed RPG inspired by the epic tale of Kalki, the final avatar of Vishnu. Set in a dystopian future, the game lets you experience thrilling storylines through the eyes of Bhairav and Ashwathama. Each character’s journey is filled with intense battles, gripping missions, and a storyline that could change the world’s fate.

Key Features

  • Dual Storylines: Dive into two captivating narratives with unique characters and missions.
  • Dynamic Combat: Engage in fast-paced battles with an array of weapons and abilities.
  • In-Game Purchases: Boost your adventure with powerful accessories and gear.
  • Ads for Rewards: Earn in-game currency by watching ads to unlock exclusive content.
  • Immersive Graphics: Experience stunning visuals and detailed environments.

Meet the Characters

Bhairav

Role: Soldier of Shambhala

Mission: Capture Sumiti and return her to Kalki Purush.

Skills: Master swordsman, stealth tactics, enhanced strength.

Ashwathama

Role: Protector of Sumiti

Mission: Defend Sumiti from Complex Soldiers to ensure the birth of Kalki God.

Skills: Archery, mystical powers, regenerative healing.

Gameplay Guide

  • Move: Use arrow keys to navigate.
  • Abilities: Use keys shown in the UI to activate abilities and consumables.
  • Interact: Press the spacebar to interact with NPCs and objects.
  • Pause: Press 'Enter' to pause the game.
  • Save: Press 'S' to save your progress.
