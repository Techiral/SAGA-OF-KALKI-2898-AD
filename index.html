About the Game

SAGA OF KALKI 2898 AD is a gripping action-adventure game inspired by the legendary tale of Kalki, the final avatar of Vishnu. Set in a dystopian future, the game offers players the chance to experience two interconnected storylines through the eyes of Bhairav and Ashwathama. Each character's journey is filled with challenges, fierce battles, and an overarching mission that could alter the world's fate.

Key Features

  • Dual Storylines: Experience two captivating narratives with different characters and missions.
  • Dynamic Combat: Engage in adrenaline-pumping battles using a variety of weapons and special abilities.
  • In-Game Purchases: Enhance your adventure with powerful accessories like the AI bot BU-JZ-1 (Bujji), advanced weaponry, vehicles, and legendary items.
  • Ads for Rewards: Watch ads to earn in-game currency and unlock exclusive content.
  • Immersive Graphics: Enjoy stunning visuals and detailed environments that bring the world of Kalki to life.

Characters

Bhairav

Role: Soldier of Shambhala

Mission: Capture Sumiti and return her to Kalki Purush.

Skills: Master swordsman, stealth tactics, enhanced strength.

Ashwathama

Role: Protector of Sumiti

Mission: Defend Sumiti from Complex Soldiers to facilitate the birth of Kalki God.

Skills: Archery, mystical powers, regenerative healing.

Gameplay Basics

  • Use arrow keys to move
  • Use keys shown in UI to use abilities and consumables
  • Use spacebar to interact with NPCs and other entities in the environment
  • Press 'Enter' to pause the game
  • Press 'S' to save your progress
